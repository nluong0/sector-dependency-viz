Sector Dependency by County

The USDA Economic Research Search (ERS) has codified “six mutually exclusive categories of economic dependence and six overlapping categories of policy-relevant themes” for each county in the United States. Economic dependence types include “farming, mining, manufacturing, Federal/State government, recreation, and non-specialized counties”. Below, you'll be able to explore which U.S. counties are dependent on which sectors as well as how the socioeconomic variables for counties in each sector-depenency group interact.
